01: THE WHY

THE "CHAT" CRISIS.

  • LLMs are trapped in browser windows and text boxes.
  • Real-world productivity requires Agency, not just answers.
  • Current agents lack professional intuition in messy environments.
02: THE GAP

MISSING ENVIRONMENTS.

  • No sandboxes for evaluating multi-step productivity logic.
  • Lack of deterministic rewards for "work-like" behavior.
  • High risk of iterating directly on production data.
03: THE SOLUTION

OPENENV HUB.

  • A realistic, secure simulator for core business tasks.
  • OpenEnv compliant interface for universal agent support.
  • Observed "Neural Thought" for safety and auditability.
04: ADVANTAGES

CORE BENEFITS.

  • Deterministic Grading: No ambiguity in success.
  • Auditability: Every thought is recorded and analyzed.
  • Scalability: Easy to add new tasks (DevOps, HR, legal).
